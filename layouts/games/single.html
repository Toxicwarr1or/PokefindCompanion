How to play

How to play

  • Click an egg to spawn a Pokémon (uses one charge).
  • Drag two of the same Pokémon together to evolve it.
  • Drag two of the same egg together to upgrade it.
  • Drag a Pokémon to the Sell zone for coins.
  • Eggs slowly recharge over time.

Egg ladder

  • Each gen has T1 → T2 → T3 (merge twos to step up).
  • Two T3 eggs promote into T1 of the next gen.
  • Gen 1 → 2 → 3 → 4 → 5 (Common all the way to Apex).
  • T3 eggs roll ~70% S3, 25% S2, 5% S1.

Aura Eggs

  • Aura Shards drop only from Gen 2+ eggs (basic eggs don't).
  • Shards are gen-stamped; same-gen Shards merge into a Fragment, then an Aura Egg of that gen.
  • Aura Eggs share the same Pokémon pool but drop coins worth more per gen.

Coins from Aura Eggs

  • Aura Eggs sometimes drop a Coin Fragment with a multiplier: G2 ×1, G3 ×2, G4 ×4, G5 ×8.
  • Frag → 1 → 10 → 50 → 100 coins, multiplied by the stamp.
  • Coins only merge with the same multiplier.

Berry Bush

  • Buy a Berry Bush for 200 coins. Each bush is locked to one of 5 species (random per purchase).
  • Each bush has 6 charges, no regen — after the last harvest it wilts away.
  • Each click drops a T1 berry of that bush's species.
  • Merge two same-species, same-tier berries to grow the next berry in the chain (up to T6).
  • Sell prices: 3 / 10 / 30 / 100 / 300 / 1000 coins (T1–T6).

Sell prices (Pokémon)

  • Gen 1 — 2 / 10 / 60
  • Gen 2 — 4 / 20 / 120
  • Gen 3 — 7 / 35 / 210
  • Gen 4 — 12 / 60 / 360
  • Gen 5 — 20 / 100 / 600
  • Aura — 5 / 25 / 150

Sell prices (Aura items)

  • Shard / Fragment / Egg base — 3 / 8 / 25
  • ×1 G2, ×2 G3, ×4 G4, ×8 G5

Season Pass

Earn XP from every merge and from completing Special Offers. Each tier unlocks a reward — coins, generators, boxes, or rare assets.
