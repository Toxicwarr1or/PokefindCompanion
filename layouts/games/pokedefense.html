How to play
  • Click a tower card, then click a grass tile to place it.
  • Click a placed tower to upgrade or sell.
  • Press Send Wave to start the next wave — early sends pay a 10-coin bonus.
  • Enemies that reach the base cost you a life. Lose all 20 and the run ends.
  • Types follow the standard chart. Some towers ignore resistances (see the tower card).
