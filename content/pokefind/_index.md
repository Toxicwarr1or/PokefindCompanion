Pokefind is a Minecraft network running several connected gamemodes. Each gamemode is its own world (or set of worlds), with its own rules, NPCs, quests, and economy.

This section is the orientation guide: pick a gamemode from the sidebar to see what it is, what regions it contains, and what each region is "about" thematically.

## Currently documented

- **PokeWorld** — the flagship Pokémon-trainer gamemode. Five regions (Kyoto, Jataro, Haikou, Shiloh, Zeinova), each with their own quests and potential stories.

More gamemode pages will land here over time.
