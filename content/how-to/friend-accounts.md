On **Minecraft Bedrock** you can join Pokéfind by adding one of the server's bot accounts as a Microsoft friend. Once the friend request is accepted, the server pops up in your **Worlds** tab the same way a friend's LAN world would.

> Friend accounts are constantly changing — **check the Discord for the most up-to-date list**. The accounts below are a starting point and the list may be ahead/behind on Discord.

## How it works

1. On your console or Bedrock device, open the Microsoft / Xbox app or in-game friends list and **add one of the bot accounts below as a friend**.
2. Wait a moment for the friend request to be accepted. The bot then shows up in your **Friends** tab inside Minecraft.
3. Open Minecraft → Play → **Worlds**. Pokéfind appears there as a Survival world named **"Pokefind"** (it's the bot's hosted world).
4. Select that world to join.

> Some accounts have a **full friend list** at any given time. If you don't see the bot in your Friends tab and the **Pokefind** world doesn't show up under Worlds after adding one, **add the next friend** in the list and try again.
