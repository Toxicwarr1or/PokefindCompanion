title: "Pokedex"

The Pokedex covers every Pokemon currently obtainable on Pokefind. Each species page has tabs for forms (Anniversary, Alolan, Galarian, unique formes) and skins (Shiny and seasonal/event variants). Cards on this page show base species; the +N forms badge counts only true forms.
