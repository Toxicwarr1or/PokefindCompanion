---
title: "Pokédex"
subtitle: "Pokéfind species reference, with all skin variants and regional anniversary forms"
---

The Pokédex covers every Pokémon currently obtainable on Pokéfind. Each species page has tabs for forms (Anniversary, Alolan, Galarian, unique formes) and skins (Shiny and seasonal/event variants). Cards on this page show base species; the +N forms badge counts only true forms.
