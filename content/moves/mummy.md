## Description

Pokemon making contact with this Pokemon have their Ability changed to Mummy. Does not affect Pokemon with the As One, Battle Bond, Comatose, Disguise, Gulp Missile, Ice Face, Multitype, Mummy, Power Construct, RKS System, Schooling, Shields Down, Stance Change, Tera Shift, Zen Mode, or Zero to Hero Abilities.

## Mechanics on Pokéfind

Listed in the Pokéfind ability registry but currently has **no in-battle effect** — the battle simulator does not implement the trigger. Treat as flavor only until the behaviour is added.
