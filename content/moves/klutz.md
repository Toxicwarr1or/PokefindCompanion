## Description

This Pokemon's held item has no effect. This Pokemon cannot use Fling successfully. Macho Brace, Power Anklet, Power Band, Power Belt, Power Bracer, Power Lens, and Power Weight still have their effects.

## Mechanics on Pokéfind

Listed in the Pokéfind ability registry but currently has **no in-battle effect** — the battle simulator does not implement the trigger. Treat as flavor only until the behaviour is added.
