## Description

On switch-in, this Pokemon's Attack and Speed are halved for 5 turns. During the effect, if this Pokemon uses a generic Z-Move based on a special move, its Special Attack is halved during damage calculation.

## Mechanics on Pokéfind

Listed in the Pokéfind ability registry but currently has **no in-battle effect** — the battle simulator does not implement the trigger. Treat as flavor only until the behaviour is added.
