## Description

This Pokemon's weight is halved, rounded down to a tenth of a kilogram. This effect is calculated after the effect of Autotomize, and before the effect of Float Stone. A Pokemon's weight will not drop below 0.1 kg.

## Mechanics on Pokéfind

Listed in the Pokéfind ability registry but currently has **no in-battle effect** — the battle simulator does not implement the trigger. Treat as flavor only until the behaviour is added.
