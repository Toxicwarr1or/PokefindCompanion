## Description

Pokemon making contact with this Pokemon have their Ability swapped with this one. Does not affect Pokemon with the Abilities As One, Battle Bond, Comatose, Commander, Disguise, Embody Aspect, Hunger Switch, Ice Face, Illusion, Multitype, Neutralizing Gas, Poison Puppeteer, Power Construct, Protosynthesis, Quark Drive, RKS System, Schooling, Shields Down, Stance Change, Tera Shell, Tera Shift, Teraform Zero, Wonder Guard, Zen Mode, or Zero to Hero.

## Mechanics on Pokéfind

Listed in the Pokéfind ability registry but currently has **no in-battle effect** — the battle simulator does not implement the trigger. Treat as flavor only until the behaviour is added.
