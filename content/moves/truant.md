## Description

This Pokemon skips every other turn instead of using a move. If this Pokemon replaces a Pokemon that fainted during end-of-turn effects, its first turn will be skipped.

## Mechanics on Pokéfind

Functions on Pokéfind as described above. Implemented in the battle simulator.
