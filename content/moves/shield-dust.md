## Description

This Pokemon is not affected by the secondary effect of another Pokemon's attack. Attacks with secondary effects that are prevented include those with a chance (even 100%) to paralyze, sleep, freeze, burn, poison, confuse, cause this Pokemon to flinch, cause this Pokemon's stat stages to be lowered, as well as Anchor Shot, Eerie Spell, Fling, Psychic Noise, Salt Cure, Spirit Shackle, Syrup Bomb, and Throat Chop. The effect of Sparkling Aria is prevented if this Pokemon is the only target. Secondary effects added by King's Rock, Razor Fang, and the Poison Touch, Stench, and Toxic Chain Abilities are also prevented against this Pokemon.

## Mechanics on Pokéfind

Functions on Pokéfind as described above. Implemented in the battle simulator.
