## Description

This Pokemon is immune to Ground-type moves and restores 1/4 of its maximum HP, rounded down, when hit by a Ground-type move.

## Mechanics on Pokéfind

**Not yet implemented** on Pokéfind. The ability has no entry in the battle simulator and currently has no in-battle effect. Treat as flavor only until support is added.
