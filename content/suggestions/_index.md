## What we look for

- **Corrections** to factual claims, stats, locations, sprites, models, quest details.
- **Missing entries** — Pokémon, items, NPCs, quests, side content.
- **New guide ideas** for the [How To](/how-to/) section.
- **Bug reports** for the site itself (broken links, layout issues, model viewer not loading).

We can't promise a turnaround time, but every submission gets read.
